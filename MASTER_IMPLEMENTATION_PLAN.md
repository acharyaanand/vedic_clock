## 8. LOCAL MEAN TIME CORRECTION
LMT = Standard Time - (Standard Meridian - Birth Longitude) × 4 minutes
India Standard Meridian = 82.5°E

Examples from reports:
- Mangalore (74.50E): (82.5-74.5)×4 = 32 min → "Std - 31 Min" ✓
- Amritsar (74.51E): (82.5-74.51)×4 = 31.96 min → "Std - 31 Min" ✓

- Kannur (75.21E): (82.5-75.21)×4 = 29.16 min → "Std - 29 Min" ✓

## 9. YOGI POINT CALCULATION
Yogi Point = Sun longitude + Moon longitude + 93°20' (mod 360)
- Ashley: Sun=280.39+Moon=247.65+93.33 = 621.37 → 261.37° = Purvashada ✓
- Sandeep: Sun=171.67+Moon=182.90+93.33 = 447.90 → 87.90° = Punarvasu ✓

Yogi Planet = Lord of the Nakshatra at Yogi Point
Duplicate Yogi = Lord of the sign at Yogi Point
Avayogi = Nakshatra that is 6th from Yogi Star

## 10. ATMA KARAKA (Jaimini System)
Atma Karaka = planet with highest degree (ignoring minutes/seconds)
among Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn
(Rahu counted in some systems, retrograde counted differently)

## 11. LAGNA ARUDA (PADA LAGNA)
Pada Lagna = Mirror of Lagna lord's position
- Count from Lagna to Lagna Lord's house = N
- Count same N from Lagna Lord's house
- That sign = Pada Lagna (Arudha)

### Rajju Classification (5 groups):
- Siro Rajju (Head): Mrigasira, Chitra, Dhanishtha (odd padas only)
- Kantha Rajju (Neck): Rohini, Hasta, Shravan
- Uras Rajju (Chest): Krittika, Uttaraphalguni, Uttarashada
- Kati Rajju (Waist): Bharani, Purva Phalguni, Purvashada
- Pada Rajju (Feet): Ashwini, Pushya, Swati, Revati, Purva/Uttara Bhadra

### Yoni (Animal) Pairs for compatibility:
Horse+Horse=best, Elephant+Elephant=best, etc.
Opposite gender of same animal = excellent
Natural enemies = poor
