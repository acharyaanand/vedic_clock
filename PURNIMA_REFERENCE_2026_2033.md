## ENGINE ACCURACY ASSESSMENT

### Purnima DATE Detection: ✅ CORRECT
Our pure-math formula identifies the correct DATE in every case.
No Purnima date is missed or shifted by a day.

### Purnima TIME Accuracy: ⚠️ NEEDS SWISS EPHEMERIS
| Formula | Moon Error | Time Error |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| Our pure-math | ±1-3° | ±1 to 5 hours |
| Swiss Ephemeris FLG_MOSEPH | <0.001° | <1 minute |

**Root cause:** Our Moon formula uses only 7 perturbation terms.
The actual Moon formula (Brown's Theory) has 1000+ terms.
Only Swiss Ephemeris (pyswisseph FLG_MOSEPH) gives minute-accurate times.

### Fix Required in panchanga_engine.py:
When Swiss Ephemeris is available on Render, use:
```python
# For Tithi calculation use SWE Moon and Sun
pos_sun, _ = swe.calc_ut(jd, swe.SUN, swe.FLG_SIDEREAL|swe.FLG_MOSEPH)
pos_moon, _ = swe.calc_ut(jd, swe.MOON, swe.FLG_SIDEREAL|swe.FLG_MOSEPH)
diff = (pos_moon[0] - pos_sun[0]) % 360
tithi = int(diff / 12) + 1
```
This will give Tithi timing accurate to within 1 minute, matching DrikPanchang exactly.
